## What is Mobility 4?

Mobility 4 is a comprehensive **field operations management platform** built for consumer goods companies, distributors, and field sales teams. It provides a centralized dashboard to coordinate promoters and supervisors working across multiple clients, regions, and establishments.

The platform connects back-office administrators with field workers in real time — enabling route planning, visit tracking, form completion, inventory management, and performance reporting all in one place.

## How it works

Mobility 4 follows a hierarchical operational model:

1. **Clients** are the companies or brands being served in the field.
2. **Regions** group geographic areas under each client.
3. **Establishments** are the physical locations (stores, points of sale) visited by field workers.
4. **Routes** bundle establishments into daily visit plans assigned to users.
5. **Users** (promoters, supervisors, administrators) carry out field operations and report back through the platform.

## Platform architecture

Mobility 4 is a React single-page application backed by a REST API. Authentication is handled via Firebase. All API calls use Bearer token authorization.
